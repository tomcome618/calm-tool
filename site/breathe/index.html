Frequently asked questions

4-7-8 breathing is a relaxation technique developed by Dr. Andrew Weil. It works by extending your exhale, which activates your parasympathetic nervous system. Inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s. Especially effective for falling asleep and managing acute stress.
Box breathing (also called square breathing) is used by Navy SEALs, athletes, and first responders. Equal counts for all phases create a steady rhythm that lowers heart rate and sharpens focus.
