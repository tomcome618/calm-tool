Frequently Asked Questions

How do the breathing exercises work?expand_more

Choose a pattern like 4-7-8 or Box Breathing, then follow the animated circle. It guides each inhale and exhale — no counting needed. Each session helps activate your parasympathetic nervous system for natural stress relief.

Is the burnout assessment accurate?expand_more

Our self-assessment uses 15 questions based on established burnout research. It provides useful insight into your stress levels, but is not a clinical diagnosis. For professional help, the 988 crisis line is always available.

Do I need to create an account?expand_more

No. CalmTool requires no registration, no email, and no personal information. Your tool settings stay in your browser — we do not collect or store any user data.

Can I use CalmTool on my phone?expand_more

Yes. All tools work on any device with a modern browser — phone, tablet, or desktop. No app installation required.
